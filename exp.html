COSTUS SPECTABILIS

Costus spectabilis is an African gregarious species of terrestrial shootless herb, low and perennial. It can be recognised by its four, sometimes three spreading leaves usually forming a cruciform rosette flat on the ground, with an inflorescence at the centre producing large yellow astonishingly beautiful flowers.

Costus spectabilis is edible as it was a snack in some part of Nigeria long ago. It also has numerous medicinal benefits. Unlike many herbs, it has a fruity taste and can be eaten raw. Today, Nigerians have used it in making stir fry, salads and smoothies.
